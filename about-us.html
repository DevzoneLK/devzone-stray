Devzone, a fast-growing IT company in Sri Lanka that excels in emerging technologies, was founded in 2018. Devzone also offers customized end-to-end digital solution services based on immersing technologies, artificial intelligence, and machine learning for the convenience of day-to-day business operations. Devzone offers its clients cutting-edge and futuristic IT solutions that are designed with quick innovation thanks to a team of more than 20 industry experts with backgrounds in a variety of areas. The Devzone headquarters, which are in Gampaha, offer a positive customer and employee culture environment where all of their innovative creations get produced.
